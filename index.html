Projects

Study App

Gallery

Team Size: 4
Event: Hack NJIT 2017 (won a sponsor prize)
Time Taken: 24 hours
Goal: Create an app that makes studying easier and more fun for students

Features:
- User can enter study mode for a certain amount of time. The first time the user does this, he or she is asked for do not disturb permissions as well as SMS permissions. This is because during study mode, the app will block the sounds of all notifications, as well as send any offending texters a message stating they are studying, and will be back in x amount of time, based on how much time is left in study mode.
- Based on how long the user studied, he or she gains experience points, and can level up. Achievements can also be earned, and turn gold after the user earns them.
- The user can also go to schedule mode, where they can choose a date on the calendar and put in their schedule for the week. Based on how many hours of classes there are, the user can gauge how much time they could/should spend on schoolwork per week. The class hours per week, as well as current study hours in the week to date, are displayed on the home page.
- Statistics mode displays the user's most hours studied in a week, as well as lifetime study hours.
- All of the data is retained, so after closing the app or shutting the device off, all of the achievements, experience points, level, schedule, and statistics are saved, and will still be there on the next run.

What's Next: Data analytics, more statistics, better schedule builder interface, customizable UI, options menu
